About iSKATER

Hi I'm Felicia!

I'm the creator of iSKATER & your new figure skating bestie! I was only 4 years old when I started the sport & 6 years old when I started competing. Nowadays I just skate for fun & enjoy coaching others. My vision behind iSKATER is a website where everyone all over the world can have access to free figure skating tutorials & that everyone can learn no matter what! This passion project is also a way for me to still share my love for the sport even if I don't participate in competitions & shows anymore. I hope you'll love this website just as much as I do, and feel free to contact me if you have any questions! / Felicia
